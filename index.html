WHO WE ARE

A carrier built
around the item,
not the invoice.

Zaldana Transportation started in 2021 with two trucks and a simple rule: treat every refrigerator, sofa and hospital bed like it's going into our own home. Today our crews cover the lower 48, running dedicated routes for retailers, contractors, hospitals and families who need something delivered — not just dropped off.

We're not a marketplace of independent movers. Every driver is W-2 employed, background-checked and trained on our own protocols for glass, appliances and clinical equipment, so the crew that answers your call is the crew that shows up at your door.

FAQ

Questions before
you book.

Which areas do you deliver to?

We run scheduled routes across all 48 contiguous states, with dedicated lanes in major metro areas and rural delivery on request.

Do you handle installation, or just drop-off?

Both. Standard service is room-of-choice placement; for appliances, windows and medical equipment we also offer basic hookup, leveling and packaging removal.

How far in advance should I book?

Most single-item deliveries can be scheduled within 3–5 business days. Multi-stop or contractor rollouts typically need 1–2 weeks lead time.

What happens if something is damaged in transit?

Every shipment is covered for full replacement value at no extra cost. Claims are reviewed within 48 hours of pickup or delivery.

Can you coordinate directly with a job site or building manager?

Yes — dispatch can confirm loading-dock windows, freight elevator reservations and site contacts ahead of delivery day.
